AliExpress Invoice Downloader

This extension helps you download official invoices for your AliExpress orders.

How to use:

  1. Click the extension icon and press "Log in to AliExpress".
  2. In the opened tab, log into your AliExpress account.
  3. After logging in, return to the extension popup and click "Allow access".
  4. Now, on any AliExpress order list or order detail page, you will see a "下载发票" (Download Invoice) button next to each order.
  5. Click the button to download the invoice as a PDF.
Note: The extension uses your logged-in session to fetch invoices directly from AliExpress. It does not store or transmit your credentials or cookies to any external server.
